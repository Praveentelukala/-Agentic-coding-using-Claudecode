Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our expense tracking service:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register
  • Expense data: Amount, category, date, and description of expenses you log
  • Usage data: Basic analytics on how you use the app to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To store and display your expense records
  • To generate spending reports and analytics
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve app functionality based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share data with service providers who assist us in operating our application, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the app or via email.
